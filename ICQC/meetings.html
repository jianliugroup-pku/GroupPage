Satellite meetings

The ICQC main conference will be held in Beijing during June 8-13, 2015. It is organized by Prof. Zhigang Shuai (Tsinghua University). Please see http://www.icqc2015.org/ for more information on other ICQC satellite meetings.

Two satellite workshops will take place at the Institue of Theoretical and Computational Chemistry of the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering of Peking University (PKU).

  • Recent advances in quantum dynamics and thermodynamics of complex systems.
    • Date: June 4-7.
    City: Beijing.
    Organizers: Jian Liu and Yi Qin Gao.
  • New frontiers of relativistic quantum chemistry.
    • Date: June 13-16.
    City: Beijing.
    Organizer: Wenjian Liu

Six other satellite workshops will be held before and after the 15th ICQC main conference.

  • The Frontiers of Computational Catalysis.
    • Date: May 31 - June 2.
    City: Beijing.
    Organizer: Jun Li.
  • Recent advances in electronic structure theory.
    • Date: June 1-6.
    City: Nanjing.
    Organizer: Shuhua Li.
  • Theoretical challenges in small molecule dynamics.
    • Date: June 2-6.
    City: Dalian.
    Organizers: Donghui Zhang, Keli Han and Millard Alexander.
  • Modeling and Simulation of Biological and Macromolecular Systems
    • Date: June 4-6.
    City: Changchun.
    Organizers: Jiali Gao and Hongxing Zhang.
  • The Chemical Bonds at the 21th Century.
    • Date: June 14-18.
    City: Xiamen.
    Organizer: Wei Wu.
  • Advances in Modeling of Nano Materials.
    • Date: June 14-17.
    City: Hefei.
    Organizers: Jinlong Yang and Yi Luo.
  • Novel computational methods for quantitative electronic structure calculations.
    • Date: June 16-20.
    City: Kobe, Japan.
    Organizers: Seiichiro Ten-no and Kimihiko Hirao.
