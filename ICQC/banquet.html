Banquet

The banquet will be arranged at Xihe Yayuan Peking Duck Restaurant (羲和雅苑) on Saturday (June 6) evening. It takes about 20 minutes to walk from the auditorium to the restaurant directly. You can also take subway from "East Gate of Peking University" station to "Zhongguancun" station and then walk there. Below is the route from Chemistry Building, Peking University to Xihe Yayuan (Sali Courtyard).
Xihe Yayuan is well received by people in Beijing for its decorated interior and tasty dishes. The restaurant features roast duck cooked with modern twist of the traditional recipe. Besides, it serves a variety of food from different regions of China.
