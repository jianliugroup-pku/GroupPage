Notes:

  • Each lecture will be limited to 40 minutes including 5 minutes for questions.
  • The auditorium is Room A204, Chemistry Building.
  • The poster session will be held at Room A104, Chemistry Building.
  • The buffet lunch will be free at the 1898 cafe for all registered participants on June 4-6. Besides, restaurants around Zhongguanyuan are also recommended in the map of lunch spots if one wants to take care of the lunch for himself/herself.
  • Before the poster session on June 4, the buffet dinner will be available at the TIME western restaurant (时光西餐厅) (on the 2nd floor of Building #2 of Zhongguan Xinyuan Hotel), which will be free for all registered participants.
  • The banquet will begin at 18:30 on Saturday (June 6) at Xihe Yayuan Peking Duck Restaurant (羲和雅苑烤鸭坊).
  • Participants will take care of the dinner by themselves on June 5 (Friday). There are many restaurants around three nearby subway stations (Wudaokou, Zhongguancun and Renmin University), where you can enjoy your dinner after the daytime programs. For details please take a look at the map of dinner spots.
