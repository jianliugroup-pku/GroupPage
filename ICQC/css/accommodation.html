Accommodation

The satellite workshop will take place at the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University (PKU). It is relatively easy to find a building called Founder near the subway station 'East Gate of Peking University', and the chemistry building is right behind it.

Maps display some decent hotels nearby, the auditorium of the satellite workshop RAQDT and that of the ICQC main conference, and the campus of Peking University. Below is a table for the hotels around PKU. In the table, four hotels labeled with "**" are most convenient for attending the satellite workshop, and they are close to the ICQC main conference too. The maps provided for each hotel show how to get to the auditorium. (You can also find the maps from the "**" hotels to the ICQC main conference.) Both the google map page and the pdf file can be zoomed in and out for your convenience. Please make your reservation from the link of the hotel. The Chinese address card of the hotel is supplied in the table. Please print it and show it to the taxi driver.


<< Click map to enlarge >>

Zhongguanyuan Hotel (the first one in the table) is the closest to the workshop auditorium. If you will attend both the workshop and the ICQC main conference, Wenjin Hotel (the second one in the table) is perhaps the best choice as it is located between the auditorium of the satellite workshop (RAQDT) in Peking University and that of the ICQC main conference in Tsinghua University.

In case that you will need to go to the auditorium by taxi, here is the Chinese address card (its png version).

请送我去北京大学化学楼。
地址：海淀区颐和园路5号东门（4号地铁北京大学东门站往东50米）。
Please take me to the Chemistry Building of Peking University.

NOTE: If you have any trouble making your hotel reservation, please contact us, with detailed information including the hotel name, dates you would like to stay, room type and so on.
