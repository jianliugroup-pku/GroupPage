Postdocs

Please feel free to contact us for information on post-doctoral positions.

Postdoc fellowship programs for the one who has obtained his/her PhD in China:

  1. 博雅博士后项目（每年1-3月以及9-10月提交申请材料。2025年：1月1日至2月28日；9月1日至10月20日）
  2. 博士后创新人才支持计划
  3. 分子青年科学家（北京大学特聘副研究员）

Postdoc fellowshop programs for international postdocs:

  1. Boya postdoc fellowship program for international postdocs. [ Application periods 2025: January 1 to Feburary 28 (Round 1); September 1 to October 10 (Round 2) ]
  2. International BMS Fellowship (IBMSF) program for international postdocs. [ Open Year-Round. ]

Peking University also offers the BMS Junior Fellowship for both domestic and international postdocs. The application deadlines are slightly later than those of the Boya postdoc fellowship every year.
