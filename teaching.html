2025年北京大学量子分子动力学暑期学校开展通知

Announcement for the 2025 Peking University Quantum Molecular Dynamics Summer School

由北京大学化学与分子工程学院理论与计算化学研究所主办的2025年量子分子动力学暑期学校将于2025年7月18-21日进行，由4~6位教授英文授课，主要面向数理基础良好的本科生和低年级研究生。随着计算机性能与人工智能的发展，量子分子动力学对于揭示化学关键过程机理、预言化学材料体系关键性质起到愈发深刻的作用。本暑期学校旨在为对原子核量子效应、原子核与电子运动耦合效应有着浓厚兴趣的学生搭建一个互相交流的平台，让已有适当基础的学生了解理论与计算化学中量子分子动力学的基础知识和前沿方向进展。欢迎学过初级量子力学的全国各地学生参加！

Organized by the Institute of Theoretical and Computational Chemistry, College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University, the 2025 Peking University Quantum Molecular Dynamics Summer School will be held from July 18 to July 21, 2025. Four to six professors will deliver the lectures in English.

With advances in computational power and artificial intelligence, quantum molecular dynamics plays an increasingly important role in elucidating key chemical processes and predicting critical properties of chemical and material systems. The summer school aims to provide a platform for students with strong interests in nuclear quantum effects and coupled electron-nuclear dynamics to exchange ideas. It covers the fundamentals and cutting-edge developments in the field of quantum molecular dynamics. Students who have learned elementary quantum mechanics are welcome to participate!
