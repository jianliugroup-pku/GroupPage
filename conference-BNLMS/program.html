Poster abstract submission

All talks are invited only. Participants who are interested in presenting posters may submit their abstracts. The maximum dimensions are 90 cm (width) by 120 cm (height).

Guideline and Rules

The deadline of the poster abstract submission is July 28, 2018. Authors should ensure that the topic is relevant to the topics of the symposium. Abstracts prepared in the template format (Download the Abstract Template) are highly encouraged. Each abstract is limited to one page. Please send your abstract to yiss18 (AT) pku.edu.cn or quanhui (AT) pku.edu.cn.
