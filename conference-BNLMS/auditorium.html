Auditorium

The symposium will take place at the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University (PKU). The auditorium is at Room A204 for the first day (Aug 4), and is moved to Room A717 for the second and third days (Aug 5-Aug 6). Both the two rooms are in the region A of the chemistry building. It is relatively easy to find a building called Founder near the subway station 'East Gate of Peking University', and the chemistry building is right behind it.
